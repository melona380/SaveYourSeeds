Are you looking for easy, creative ways to disperse your seeds? We have compiled a list of simple do's and don'ts when finding places to do so.

  1. Find a public place where your magic beans will grow. The only requirement is the desired discarding area must contain soil. Cannabis will grow in any type of soil known to man.
  2. Do not discard anywhere that is regularly mowed or sprayed with herbicides. By doing this you will not be able to appreciate the full lifecycle of the plants.
  3. It is desirable to plant somewhere conspicuous enough where the six-foot plant can be seen in all its glory by any onlooker.
  4. Here are some good places to toss your seeds:
    1. Dirt-filled gaps between concrete pads or buildings.
    2. The edge of tree lines where people stop mowing.
    3. Roadside ditches work well too, and planting can be as easy as throwing the seeds out your window.
  5. The ideal time to disperse your seeds is in spring, or during the rainy season on the West Coast, so plants may germinate naturally.
  6. Try not to plant the seeds in a place that is already overrun with weeds. Although cannabis is a weed itself and can grow in almost anything, by planting the seeds in someplace weed-free, they will at least have a fighting chance to grow into big mature plants.
