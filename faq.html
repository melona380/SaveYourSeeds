Q: How and where should I dispose of my seeds?
A: Take a look at our "Tips" page for inspiration.

Q: Is this a legalization campaign?
A: We support sensible cannabis policies regarding the use and cultivation of marijuana. Our focus is on promoting awareness for current legislation as well as supporting and advancing informed, logical discussion.

Q: I've been saving my seeds. Can I donate them to your group?
A: No, our group exists to manage and promote this website. As a group, we do not collect or distribute marijuana seeds. If you happen to acquire some, we encourage you to dispose of them in nature where they belong.
